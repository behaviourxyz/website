X SLASH OBJECTS
REFRAMING PRODUCT DESIGN IN A MATERIAL WORLD

BEHAVIOUR® has been partnering with New York based agency SLASH OBJECTS to help them develop brand strategy and create new pathways to growth through a series of accelerator sessions.

Founded by designer and architect Arielle Assouline-Lichten, Slash Objects works to transform how humans experience the world through design. Working in very close partnership, and through dynamic dialogue, Slash Objects and BEHAVIOUR® have arrived at a set of actionable and interconnected business and brand strategies that will push the business forward.

Their development of high end products and furniture using recycled materials is another opportunity for the team to support innovators and entrepreneurs with a focus on solving the challenges faced by the world’s climate and economy.
