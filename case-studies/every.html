All Protein. No Animal.

San Francisco, CA

  • Strategy
  • Brand

The Company – Clara Foods, headquartered in San Francisco, was a market leader in engineering, manufacturing and formulating animal-free animal proteins as ingredients for the global food and beverage industry.

The Challenge – Create a brand that can inspire immediate investment, galvanize the internal team and key external audiences, and build Clara Foods a broad platform from which it can apply its scientific breakthroughs and share a bold vision for global food production.

/
/
/
/
/

Stragey –

Through workshops and one-to-one conversations, using dynamic collaborative tools, we gained a deep understanding of the business and its ambition. We distilled this into a brand strategy built on the central idea of food production that minimises resource use and makes a positive impact, with the world’s first animal-free protein.

/

/
/

Brand –

From naming through to every element of the graphic execution, we created a brand world that sets out a distinct visual and tonal personality within an increasingly crowded marketplace.
